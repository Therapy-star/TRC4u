Welcome to The Rebellion Club

TRC is a safe and supportive environment designed to help children ages 6-13 navigate through their emotions, build resilience, and develop coping skills in a fun and engaging way. Remember that this place is run by kids your age!

About Us

Our mission at TRC is to empower young rebels to overcome life's challenges. Our team of licensed therapists, educators, and child development experts work together to create personalized therapy experiences that are both effective and enjoyable.

Our Services

  • Individual Therapy Sessions
  • Group Therapy
  • Parent therapy
  • Parent and child(children)
